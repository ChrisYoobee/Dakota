About Us

Dakota is an awesome cowboy 'n' Cowgirl themed party bar in Wellington NZ. Playing country party and classic rock music.

Dakota is simply the best place for a fun night out in Wellington. With the winning combination of Friendly staff, great service, drinks deals and amazing music this place is like no other. If you're looking for a night out or place to hold your next function then here's where you want to be.

We hold FREE Line dancing lessons every Wednesday, and also have FREE mechanical bull rides on our bull Buck Norris every Tuesday, Thursday, Friday and Saturday night.
